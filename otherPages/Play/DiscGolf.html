Jones Park

If you are looking for the Disc Golf Capital of the World, you found it! Emporia, Kansas is often referred to “Disc Golf Disneyland” by players who come here from around the world to play our five 18-hole and three 9-hole disc golf courses. There are five additional courses within 10-30 miles. Emporia is home to the Glass Blown Open, the largest disc golf tournament in the world! 1,700 disc golfers from 47 states and 10 countries flock to Emporia for a week filled with play, fun, camaraderie, new friends, and competition. Community events include a craft beer festival, mini disc golf tournaments, a Swedish bonfire and a downtown block party.

Twin Rivers Winery & Gourmet Shoppe

If you’re a fan of craft beer, wine, or spirits, Emporia has something to suit your palette – all locally made.

Sample a beer flight at the award-winning Radius Brewery. Located in historic Downtown Emporia and voted one of the top Kansas breweries, Radius features a rotating selection of delectable craft beer. Most brews are available in growlers and crowlers to take home.

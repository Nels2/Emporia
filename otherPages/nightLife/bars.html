Always Filled with Adventure.

You’ll find great nightlife options throughout Emporia, but when it comes to the best excitement, downtown is the place to be. When the sun goes down, the streets fill up with latenight diversions, including scotch bars, lounges and live music. There are many restaurants, neighborhood pubs and clubs within walking distance. Whether you’re looking to grab a quick bite or a romantic dinner before heading out on the town, Emporia’s nightlife options will keep you entertained well into the night.
